Games, Apps and Web Portfolio

Hi, I'm Andy Ballard and I am a web developer and 2D games programmer. I make HTML5 games that work on the web so they can be embeded in a website, and seamlessly work on desktop or mobile. They can also be packaged in an app.

I use the flexible Phaser.js framework to create games, and have extended the capabilities of the framework further in some projects. Phaser can also be used to create interactives and visuals on a website, which makes your site stand out from flat pages. Interactives could be a quiz or puzzle, or collect data and email addresses from users.

I have put together a showcase of some of the games, and demo games I have worked on, which give an idea of what I can do. I can work alone or as part of a team.

I also build hybrid Android apps using the Ionic framework and Angular.js

Biplanes Champion

A remake of the 90's single screen dog-fight game Biplane Duel. A side view of a landscape with two biplane aeroplanes that take off, perform acrobatics and duel. First to 8 points wins!

I designed the controls to mimic the tight and quick feel of the original Amiga game, even down to the engine stall if you climb to steeply.

Draw a House on a Tank game

I am making my own take on the classic turn based Tanks game. A 2D game of skill and tactics where you aim your tanks cannon and judge the shots trajectory to hit your enemy. I beefed up the idea and removed the turns, added an aiming guide, and let players "draw" they're own walls and defences to block oponents missiles.

Potential for future internet multiplay and extra weapons

Live Demo

Under the hood

Using Phaser.js, but with a custom camera routine to allow the screen to zoom out when you shoot your cannon at the distant enemy. The game has a custom destroyable bitmap landscape, and a trajectory setting joypad. Also note the bouncing bomb on level two, which calculates the surface normals against a changing landscape on collision.

LunchAlert App

In a team of three where I was the full stack developer, LunchAlert was made for a local Sandwich van business. It has since grown to be used at hundreds of business parks around the UK, including by one of the largest bakeries in the country.

The app allows marketing promotions to be dynamically pushed out to all the delivery vans customers, including push notifications.

National Award Winner!

In 2017, LunchAlert won the New sandwich product of the year award in the British Sandwich Industries award show The Sammies. This was covered in the Warrington Guardian.

Warrington Guardian Article

Technology

The App used the Ionic Framework, Angular.js and Cordova, and is on the App Store and Google Play store. It receives an instant notification when the delivery van arrives, controlled by a maching app the van driver uses. The app also shows dynamically changable content for promotions.

  

Planet Hopper

A small game demo written using the HTML5 canvas element. The rocket is controlled with the cursor keys and can explore a wrap around scrolling planet.

Space

If you venture high enough, the sky changes colour as you pass through different atmosphere layers, and eventually enter space. The game then seemlessly zooms out and you orbit the planet from space in a different game mode.

I dont currently have a demo online, but may one day convert it to Phaser and continue this game, expanding it to have missions to collect items on different planets, asteroids, underground caves and alien forests.

Asteroids

For a marketing company client I developed an Asteroids based game. You are a rocket in space holding off a fleet of attacking flying saucers. Your rocket is controlled with the cursor keys or an onscreen joystick for mobile.

Rocket Blast Demo

More than just graphics

Making games feel playable is an artform in itself, so I put some thought into the way the rocket accelerates in this game.

An air friction routine was added to give the rocket 'punchy' acceleration at low speeds, but taper off as you get faster.
